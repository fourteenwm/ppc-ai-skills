## Tone Guidelines

### DO:
- Write like a strategic advisor, not a vendor
- Use "we discovered" and "the data shows" - be objective
- Focus on implications, not just observations
- Make it actionable - what should they DO with this?
- Be specific - use actual quotes and numbers from research

### DON'T:
- Mention Google Ads, campaigns, keywords, or ad copy
- Use jargon (CTR, CPC, impression share, etc.)
- Be salesy or promotional
- Offer to do more work (this is a gift, not a pitch)
- Include unverified claims about the client

## Vertical-Specific Adjustments

### Auto Repair
- Emphasize local market dynamics
- Include review/rating comparisons prominently
- Focus on trust and transparency themes

### Property Management
- Emphasize portfolio positioning
- Include amenity and lifestyle comparisons
- Focus on resident experience themes
- Reference Fair Housing compliance where relevant
- Position as relationship-strengthening gift

### FAQ Section

**How long does this take?**
5-7 business days for the full analysis. Rush available if needed.

**What if I don't know who my competitors are?**
We can help identify them. We'll use search data, industry research, and your knowledge of who you lose customers to.

**Is this just screenshots?**
No. Screenshots are included, but the value is in the analysis - what the patterns mean, where the gaps are, and what to do about it.

**How is this different from just Googling them?**
Anyone can look at a competitor's website. This is systematic analysis across multiple competitors, with strategic frameworks, and actionable recommendations. It's not browsing - it's intelligence.

## For Property Management (Internal Use)

### Positioning for Property Management Clients

This isn't marketing copy - it's how to frame the deliverable when presenting to clients.

**Introduction:**
"We put together some market intelligence for you. We analyzed your top 5 competitors to understand how the market is positioning and where opportunities might exist."

**Key messages:**
- "Here's what everyone in your market is saying..."
- "Here's what no one is saying - and that's interesting..."
- "Here's where we think there's an opportunity..."

**Avoid:**
- "We did this analysis to sell you more services"
- "This is a standard deliverable" (make it feel special)
- Technical jargon (keep it strategic)

**Closing:**
"We thought this might be helpful as you think about positioning. Happy to discuss any of it."

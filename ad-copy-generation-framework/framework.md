# The 23-Element Ad Copy Framework

Complete reference for Google Ads ad copy principles, organized by priority tier.

---

## TIER 1: MANDATORY ELEMENTS (Always Use)

These elements should appear in every ad copy project.

| # | Element | Use | Principle | Implementation |
|---|---------|-----|-----------|----------------|
| **1** | **Keyword in Headline** | Default | Typically your H1 or H2. Searcher is 'in the right place'. | Include target keyword in first or second headline position for relevance |
| **2** | **Intent vs Specificity** | Default | Consider the traditional funnel: top, middle, bottom. Keywords and ads should narrow/compliment. | Match ad specificity to funnel stage - broad at top, specific at bottom |
| **3** | **Capitalize Each Word** | Default | Capitalize the 'First Letter Of Each Word' for improved legibility. | Use Title Case for all headlines and descriptions |
| **4** | **Call to Action** | Default | Get someone to take action. Repeat on your destination page. | Include CTA that also appears on landing page (continuity) |
| **23** | **Continuity** | Default | If you mention something specific in your ad copy, a feature, a price, an outcome, etc – show it on your destination page. | Ad claims must be visible on landing page |

---

## TIER 2: PREFERRED ELEMENTS (Strongly Recommended)

Use these in most campaigns for best performance.

| # | Element | Use | Principle | Implementation |
|---|---------|-----|-----------|----------------|
| **11** | **Punctuation** | Preferred | Use *symbols* - !, ?, %, £/$/€ - to stand-out from other advertisers. | Add symbols strategically to catch attention |
| **12** | **Timely** | Preferred | Use recency to persuade and convince searchers you are the right option. | Include current year, "latest", "new", urgency language |
| **15** | **Features and Benefits** | Preferred | Highlight what it *is* and what it *does*. The positive outcome. | Show both the feature AND the benefit it provides |
| **16** | **Single Focus** | Preferred | Avoid the temptation to include *all* features and benefits. Focus on the most appropriate for this searcher/audience. | Pick most relevant feature for each headline, don't list everything |
| **17** | **Problems and Solutions** | Preferred | Present the solution your product/service provides. Surveying customers is a good source of 'solution' inspiration. | Address customer pain points and present your solution |
| **20** | **Pre-Qualify** | Preferred | Use pre-qualifying language in your ads (and keywords). Weed out the wasted clicks. | Include qualifying criteria to filter unqualified clicks |

---

## TIER 3: OPTIONAL ELEMENTS (Use Strategically)

Apply these tactically based on campaign goals and audience.

| # | Element | Use | Principle | Implementation |
|---|---------|-----|-----------|----------------|
| **5** | **Imperative Verb** | Optional | Tells someone to *do* something. A command. | Use action words: Act, Book, Call, Get, Try, Shop, Order |
| **6** | **Competitor Comparisons** | Optional | You don't have to *name* your competitor to call them out. | Reference competitors indirectly without naming them |
| **7** | **Use Repetition** | Optional | It's okay to have the keyword/key element in your headline *and* a description line too. | Repeat key concepts across headlines and descriptions |
| **8** | **Ignore H3 and/or D2** | Optional | Sometimes a bigger ad isn't better. Don't force yourself to use *all* the text space. | Skip optional headline/description slots if they weaken the ad |
| **9** | **Show Results** | Optional | Consider what the prospect *really* wants. What result are they getting from you? | Focus on outcomes, not just features or process |
| **10** | **Proximity** | Default (for local) | Mention the location in your ad. Include a local number call extension. | Always include location for local businesses |
| **13** | **DKI** | Optional | Not perfect, but can be used as a quick fix and/or for testing. | Dynamic Keyword Insertion for testing (use cautiously) |
| **14** | **Fear of Missing Out** | Optional | Countdown to an event, sale or launch. | Create urgency with time-limited offers or scarcity |
| **18** | **Social Proof** | Optional | Show authority, reviews, testimonials and trust signals. | Include ratings, review counts, testimonials |
| **19** | **Award Winning** | Optional | Talk *to* - and not *at* - your prospect. If you mention an award, make it specific to the customer's needs. | Frame awards in terms of customer benefit, not just bragging |
| **21** | **Sentiment** | Optional | Analyze top ranking competitor ads and pages for positive sentiment ideas in your own ads. | Use positive sentiment (0.8-1.0 target), analyze competitors |
| **22** | **Puns** | Optional | If you are going to use puns in your adverts - make them *very* good or *purposely* bad. Puns need to be on brand too. | Use puns strategically - must be very clever or intentionally cheesy |

## Element Details & Examples

### 1. Keyword in Headline
**Principle**: Searcher needs to know they're in the right place
**Why It Works**: Relevance signal to both user and Google Ads Quality Score

**Examples**:
- ✅ "Emergency Plumber Austin TX" (keyword in H1)
- ✅ "Expert Oil Change Service Near You" (keyword in H2)
- ❌ "Call Us Today For Service" (no keyword, vague)

### 2. Intent vs Specificity (Funnel Matching)
**Principle**: Match ad message to search intent and funnel stage
**Why It Works**: Right message at right time increases CTR and conversion

**Funnel Stages**:
- **Top (Awareness)**: Broad keywords, educational tone, "Learn more about..."
- **Middle (Consideration)**: Service-specific, comparison language, "Why choose us..."
- **Bottom (Decision)**: Urgent CTAs, pricing, "Call now", "Book today"

**Examples**:
- Top: "What Is ASE Certification?" → "Learn About Auto Repair Standards"
- Middle: "Oil change near me" → "Expert Oil Change - Synthetic or Conventional"
- Bottom: "Emergency brake repair" → "Emergency Brake Repair - Same Day Service!"

### 3. Capitalize Each Word (Title Case)
**Principle**: Improved legibility and professionalism
**Why It Works**: Stands out in search results, easier to scan

**Examples**:
- ✅ "Get Same-Day Brake Repair Today"
- ✅ "Licensed & Insured Auto Mechanics"
- ❌ "get same-day brake repair today"
- ❌ "LICENSED & INSURED AUTO MECHANICS" (all caps = shouting)

### 4. Call to Action
**Principle**: Tell people what to do next
**Why It Works**: Removes ambiguity, increases conversion rate

**Strong CTAs**: Call Now, Book Today, Get Quote, Schedule Service, Order Online, Start Free Trial

**Continuity Requirement**: If ad says "Free Quote", landing page must prominently show "Free Quote" offer

**Examples**:
- ✅ "Call Now For Same-Day Service!" + Landing page has "Call Now" button
- ✅ "Book Your Appointment Today!" + Landing page has booking form
- ❌ "Call For Pricing" + Landing page has no phone number visible

### 11. Punctuation (Symbols)
**Principle**: Stand out from competitors using symbols
**Why It Works**: Visual pattern interrupt in search results

**Effective Symbols**: ! ? $ % & - /

**Examples**:
- ✅ "Save 20% On Your First Service!"
- ✅ "24/7 Emergency Service Available?"
- ✅ "Rated 4.9★ By 2000+ Customers"
- ❌ "Save money on service" (no symbols, bland)

### 12. Timely (Recency)
**Principle**: Current = credible and relevant
**Why It Works**: Demonstrates you're active, modern, and up-to-date

**Examples**:
- ✅ "2025 ASE Certified Mechanics"
- ✅ "New: EV Battery Service Available"
- ✅ "Updated Safety Protocols" (during COVID)
- ❌ Generic claims with no timeframe

### 15. Features AND Benefits
**Principle**: Show what it IS (feature) and what it DOES (benefit)
**Why It Works**: Features prove capability, benefits sell the outcome

**Formula**: [Feature] + [Benefit]

**Examples**:
- ✅ "ASE Certified Techs" (feature) → "Get Expert Diagnosis" (benefit)
- ✅ "Lifetime Warranty" (feature) → "Never Pay Twice" (benefit)
- ✅ "Mobile Service" (feature) → "We Come To You" (benefit)
- ❌ "We have ASE certified technicians" (feature only, no benefit)

### 16. Single Focus
**Principle**: Less is more - pick the most relevant feature for this audience
**Why It Works**: Clarity beats comprehensiveness in limited ad space

**Examples**:
- ✅ "24/7 Emergency Plumbing" (focused on availability)
- ✅ "No Hidden Fees - Flat Rate Pricing" (focused on pricing transparency)
- ❌ "Licensed, Insured, 30 Years, ASE, BBB, Family Owned..." (too much, diluted)

### 17. Problems and Solutions
**Principle**: Lead with the problem, present your solution
**Why It Works**: Demonstrates you understand customer pain points

**Formula**: [Problem Acknowledgment] + [Your Solution]

**Examples**:
- ✅ "Car Won't Start? Same-Day Diagnostics"
- ✅ "Tired Of Surprise Charges? Flat Rate Pricing"
- ✅ "Can't Take Time Off Work? Mobile Repair Service"

### 20. Pre-Qualify
**Principle**: Filter out unqualified clicks to reduce wasted spend
**Why It Works**: Better quality traffic = higher conversion rate, lower CPA

**Examples**:
- ✅ "Starting At $79 Oil Change" (price qualifier)
- ✅ "Serving Houston Metro Area" (location qualifier)
- ✅ "For European Vehicles Only" (vehicle qualifier)
- ✅ "Commercial Fleet Service Available" (customer type qualifier)

### 18. Social Proof
**Principle**: Others have validated you - show it
**Why It Works**: Reduces risk perception, builds trust

**Types of Social Proof**:
- Ratings: "Rated 4.9★ By 2000+ Customers"
- Reviews: "\"Best Shop in Austin\" - Happy Customer"
- Credentials: "BBB A+ Rated Since 2010"
- Certifications: "AAA Approved Auto Repair"
- Years: "Trusted Since 1985"

### 22. Puns
**Principle**: Memorable wordplay can work IF done well
**Why It Works**: Stands out, shows personality, memorable

**Rules**:
- Must be VERY clever OR intentionally bad (no middle ground)
- Must be on-brand
- Don't overuse (1 per RSA set max)

**Examples (Auto Repair)**:
- ✅ "Brake For Quality Service" (clever wordplay)
- ✅ "We'll Steer You Right" (auto-themed)
- ✅ "Don't Let Problems Exhaust You" (intentionally cheesy)
- ❌ "We're Tired Of Bad Service" (confusing, weak)

### 23. Continuity (Critical)
**Principle**: Everything in your ad must appear on your landing page
**Why It Works**: Reduces bounce rate, improves Quality Score, builds trust

**Continuity Checklist**:
- ✅ Price mentioned in ad → Price shown on page
- ✅ "Free Quote" in ad → "Free Quote" form on page
- ✅ "24/7 Service" in ad → "24/7" prominently displayed
- ✅ "Same-Day Appointments" in ad → Booking system shows same-day availability
- ❌ "Special Offer" in ad → No offer visible on page

**Quality Score Impact**: Google penalizes ads with poor continuity (landing page relevance factor)

## Framework Application

### For Every RSA (15 Headlines + 4 Descriptions)

**Mandatory Elements to Include**:
1. ✅ Keywords in H1-H2
2. ✅ Match funnel stage (top/middle/bottom)
3. ✅ Title Case everything
4. ✅ At least 2 CTAs
5. ✅ Continuity check before launch

**Preferred Elements to Include** (aim for 4-5):
- Punctuation/symbols in 50%+ headlines
- Timely language (year, "latest", urgency)
- Features + Benefits in 4+ headlines
- Single focus per headline
- Address at least 1 problem/solution
- Pre-qualifying language where relevant

**Optional Elements to Consider**:
- 2 social proof headlines (if reviews available)
- 1 pun headline (if on-brand)
- FOMO/urgency (if time-sensitive offer)
- Proximity (always for local)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **All keywords, no benefits**: "Austin Plumber | Licensed Plumber | Plumbing Service"
✅ **Balance keywords + benefits**: "Austin Plumber | Same-Day Service | Rated 4.9★"

❌ **lowercase or ALL CAPS**: "call us today for service" or "BEST SHOP IN TOWN"
✅ **Title Case**: "Call Us Today For Service"

❌ **Vague CTAs**: "Contact us", "Learn more", "Click here"
✅ **Specific CTAs**: "Call Now", "Book Today", "Get Free Quote"

❌ **Ad promises not on landing page**: "50% Off" in ad, no discount on page
✅ **Perfect continuity**: "50% Off" in ad + "50% Off" banner on page

❌ **Feature-only copy**: "ASE Certified, Insured, 30 Years Experience"
✅ **Feature + Benefit**: "ASE Certified Mechanics - Get Expert Diagnosis"

❌ **No differentiation**: "Quality auto repair service"
✅ **Specific differentiation**: "Lifetime Warranty On Labor - Never Pay Twice"
